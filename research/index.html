Research

In my PhD I use agent-based models to look into the hypothesis that language and mindreading (a.k.a. theory of mind) may have co-evolved. More specifically, I explore under what circumstances such a co-evolution could have come off the ground, given certain sets of assumptions about how agents communicate and learn, and what cognitive biases and external pressures play a role.
