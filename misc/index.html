Miscellaneous

In my spare time I like to be involved in community garden projects. Aside from my PhD I have a post as a sessional worker with Cyrenians, where I work together with patients of the Astley Ainslie Hospital to breathe new life into their hospital garden. This is a rehabilitation hospital for people who have suffered brain injury, a stroke, orthopaedic injuries, limb amputation, or other progressive neurological disorders. In this 6-month project we will completely re-design and re-plant their garden based on the patients' needs and wishes, working together with the patients and their visitors.
Before starting this project with the Astley Ainslie Hospital, I was a patient-engagement volunteer at Cyrenians, working with patients who were staying at the acute wards of the Royal Edinburgh Hospital for Mental Health. Both in the Royal Edinburgh Community Garden which is located on the hospital grounds, and by taking some of the garden with us to the wards.
From September to December 2014 I spent 3 months in Bamenda, Cameroon, where I volunteered with Better World Cameroon.
